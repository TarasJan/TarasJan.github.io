Hello kind stranger

My name is Jan Taras. I am a Software Engineer based in Warsaw, Poland. I currently work at Tjekvik. I have previously worked at Sage and Zendesk. My primary stack is Ruby on Rails, but I do dabble in Javascript, Python and Go from time to time.

The theme of this website is space. The term has many conotations. It represents the unknown, the possibility the vastness. Though without a form in itself it is the origin of all things. Space allows us to recognize what the objects in the universe are and how they relate to each other. Space is the fabric upon which the cosmos paints its meaning.

This little chunk of space I have made just for you. So that you may see who I am and how I relate to your heart and labour. I wish you all the best on your journey kind stranger,

Safe travels 🚀
